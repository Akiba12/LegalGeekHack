Can we help?

We hope so!
We are able to assist if you live anywhere in England and Wales, have access to a computer and otherwise can't afford a lawyer.*

Immigration and asylum - Employment -Discrimination - Debt - Education rights - Family - Housing.

How does this work?

LawyerFlex is a completely online service. This means you don't have to spend time travelling to a walk-in centre or spend ages in a queue waiting for an appointment.

Once you upload your details, we'll match you with one of our available FlexLawyers who'll give you a call.

Who are we?

We are a community of full-time lawyers across the country who are able to take a look at your case and help you understand your options. We are able to fit in time to help around our normal day jobs.

We work in big and small firms and practice the area of law that you need help with.
